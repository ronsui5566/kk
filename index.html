LATEST RESEARCH 2023
The Go‑Lab Platform, an Inquiry‑learning Space: Investigation into Students’ Technology Acceptance, Knowledge Integration, and Learning Outcomes
Journal of Science Education and Technology (2023) 32:61–77
Go-Lab Mendelian Genetics Technology Acceptance Knowledge Integration

Utilizing an inquiry-learning space (ILS) via the Go-Lab platform, we investigated students’ technology acceptance, knowledge integration [KI] process, and learning outcomes. This study aimed to understand how students engage in knowledge integration tasks using an inquiry-learning space to learn Mendelian genetics and realize the relationship between student KI and domain knowledge.
